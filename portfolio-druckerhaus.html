A Strategic Partnership with German Retailer Druckerhaus24.de

Introduction

Druckerhaus24.de is a multi-domain, multi-store office hardware retailer based in Germany. The client sought a more efficient and expert Magento 2 support solution, a search that led them to approach our team directly at our Tbilisi office. Their goal was to find a partner capable of managing their complex digital infrastructure more effectively.

The Challenge:

Unsustainable Costs and Technical Complexity

The client's primary challenge was a prohibitively expensive existing support developer. This high cost structure limited their ability to invest in improvements for their technically sophisticated e-commerce platform. The system's complexity—encompassing a multi-domain and multi-store setup, multi-channel sales integrations with eBay and Amazon, and a custom ERP integration—demanded a high level of expertise that was proving financially unsustainable.

The Solution:

A High-Value Development Partnership

We proposed a new partnership model designed to provide superior support and development capabilities at a significantly lower cost. The solution involved replacing their incumbent developer with our team. This immediately decreased their development expenditure by a factor of three while simultaneously providing them with more development hours, delivering instant value and creating opportunities for future growth projects.

The Approach: Evolving from Support to Strategic Guidance

The engagement commenced with our team providing dedicated support. However, our role quickly expanded as we demonstrated a deep understanding of their business and technical needs. The client began to rely on us for strategic decisions, including guidance on selecting the best extensions and determining the best technical approach for resolving core issues. This evolution from a simple support function to a comprehensive development partner aligns with our vision of systematically dismantling project ambiguity and fostering sustained value.

Project Execution & Scope: Mastering Complexity and Modernization

Our team was tasked with managing and improving a highly customized Magento environment. Key execution initiatives included:

  • Complex Migrations: We successfully executed challenging Magento version migrations that required jumping PHP versions—a non-trivial task for a store with this level of custom functionality.
  • Frontend Modernization: In parallel with the backend upgrades, we performed a frontend migration to a Hyva theme, leveraging our certified training in this area. This was a strategic decision to drastically improve site speed and user experience.
  • Ongoing Operations: We continue to deliver robust support for their multi-store setup, multi-channel integrations, and the critical custom ERP connection to their warehouse, ensuring seamless operations.

The Results: Financial Efficiency and Dramatic Performance Gains

The primary value delivered through this collaboration is stability. For more than three years, we have functioned as a dependable, daily partner. The tangible results of this partnership are evident:

  • Cost Reduction: The client achieved a threefold decrease in development costs.
  • Performance Enhancement: Since the migration to the Hyva theme, site load time has improved by 2-3 times, now averaging around 1 second—a significant upgrade from the previous architecture.
  • Operational Stability: We have successfully maintained complex integrations with very specific business logic that would typically incur high-cost maintenance, demonstrating our operational efficiency.

Conclusion: Delivering Tangible Value and Predictable Success

This collaboration with Druckerhaus24.de showcases our core value proposition. We provided an immediate, high-impact solution to unsustainable costs and then evolved the relationship to deliver strategic improvements and tangible performance gains. By expertly navigating a complex, multi-faceted e-commerce environment, we have delivered a faster, more efficient, and cost-effective platform for our client, embodying our motto: Digital Clarity.
