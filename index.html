Advanced Magento Table Rates extension.

Need a Customization? Contact Us!

The Developers Alliance Table Rates Magento 2 module enhances the default Magento functionality for managing shipping table rates by providing a user-friendly interface for viewing and managing table rates.

Magento Wholesale extension.

Need a Customization? Contact Us!

The Developers Alliance Magento Wholesale extension is a valuable tool, showcasing configurable products all the simple products in a table grid view. This approach significantly enhances the clarity and detail of product displays, while also allowing users to easily adjust the desired quantity of the product.

Magento Abandoned Cart extension.

Need a Customization? Contact Us!

The Developers Alliance Abandoned Cart. This extension is designed to handle abandoned shopping carts. It monitors the state of a user's shopping cart and sends an email reminder to the customer if the cart is active but hasn't been updated for a certain period of time.

Magento Payze Payment gateway extension.

Need a Customization? Contact Us!

The Developers Alliance extension for PAYZE Payment Gateway. PAYZE is a way to clear and effortless online payments. We provide various technical solutions that allow you to manage your payment processes in the blink of an eye.
